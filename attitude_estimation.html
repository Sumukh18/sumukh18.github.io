Guide: Dr. Yujendra Mitikiri, IIT Tirupati

[Aug'23 - Nov'23]

Developed a MATLAB-based model to simulate the attitude kinematics and dynamics of a satellite under the influence of thruster-generated external moments.

Incorporated sensor models with indicated noise characteristics into the simulation to represent real-world sensor measurements accurately.

Implemented three different attitude estimation methods - Triad algorithm with home-tracker and star tracker sensors, q-method, and an extended attitude Kalman filter - to estimate the satellite's attitude based on sensor measurements, showcasing diverse approaches to attitude estimation.
